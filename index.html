A primitive yordle born millennia in the past, Gnar is playful, diminutive and cute until his occasional bouts of anger transform him into a massive, violent, and destructive beast. Trapped within a glacier of True Ice, Gnar was frozen for an age before he broke free, emerging to find the world much changed. These new, exotic realms only ignite his delight and curiosity, for where most fear danger, Gnar pounces to meet it head on.
  • Always go top with Gnar and build a high cs quickly, around 50 every 10 minutes
  • Don't try to engage teamfights without Mega Gnar, because it would be harder without powerful ccs
  • Early game try to poke and kite - mostly Mini Gnar
  • Mid game use your cc and mobility to start and win teamfights - mostly Mega Gnar
  • Late game try to balance poke, kite and engage, cc - Mega and Mini Gnar

  • The typical harass combo for Zed is WEQ to make the Q easier to land
  • Before you go all wait a few seconds for your energy to restore so that you dont run out of energy.
  • When being ganked by the enemy jungler and your W is down you can cast your E on enemy and it reduces the cool down
  • Early game you can use your passive to your advantage by WEQ'ing to harass your opponent
  • Remember to use your ult to avoid skill shots
